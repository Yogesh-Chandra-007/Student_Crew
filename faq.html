❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list a product?

You can list a product by clicking on 'Sell Now' from the dashboard and filling out the form.

What are Coins used for?

Coins help unlock special features, promotions, or highlights on your listings.

Is it safe to meet buyers/sellers on campus?

We recommend meeting in public spots on campus and informing a friend before you meet.

How do I report inappropriate content?

Use the 'Report Issue' option under your profile menu to flag any listings or users.

How can I reset my password?

Visit the login page and click 'Forgot Password' to receive reset instructions.
